what is speech-controlled

March 25, 2018

What is speech-controlled? For me, speech-controlled is a platform to share my ideas and speech-related projects with you. It is also an opportunity to improve my writing skills. As the name suggests, the focus is going to be on speech-based human-machine interaction. This is a very interdisciplinary field which allows us to look into many interesting topics such as real-time audio processing, speech recognition, natural language processing, user interface design and others.

I am planning to release practical guides, lessons learned and commentary on recent advancements in the field. Furthermore, I am planning to share my experience in designing and developing a real-time spoken dialog system.

If you are interested and want to know more about me checkout my about page and for notifications when new posts go live follow me on Twitter.
